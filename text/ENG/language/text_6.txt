The castle is the most dangerous place!

cavemen trolls, the worst gargoyles and evil
witches. The castle has over a million doors 
and rooms. Behind every door is unknown ... 
Behind the doors you can find
chest or gargoyle. Pass one hundred faithful
doors and find the last passage from this
valleys. Be careful with monsters! They
will guard the castle to the last.
